About Us









What We Do:

A platform intended to empower women in the IT industry which is woefully unrepresented.
The site aims to match female mentors with female mentee.



Why We Do it:

Supporting female mentors and mentees in matching interests, projects and skill sets.
By providing access to a virtual meet-up lowers the barriers to entry faced by women in their quest to develop and refine their technical skills.



How it works

  • This is a meeting place for women to meet mentors to support them
  • You complete a quick questionnaire of what skills you would like to build
  • After the survey is complete, it suggests what projects are available and what they are looking for and shows the profile of the mentor who will manage that project
  • When you find projects that you like, you can favourite them. You create an account so you can save your options
  • You can then get in contact with the mentor/mentee to explore options. ​
  • Based on your favourite options, it can suggest other suitable projects.​
